Plastic Wastes Exported from the U.S. in Recent Five Years

With A Close Look at New York City

By Yinan Dong

Published July 15, 2019

Since January 2018, China has executed the "National Sword" policy and banned the import of most plastics and other materials. Before that, the country's recycling processors dealt with nearly half of the world's recyclable waste. As a result, a lot of countries which rely on China a lot in waste disposal, including the United States, have been through a hard time to look for ways of handling a huge amount of extra waste.
